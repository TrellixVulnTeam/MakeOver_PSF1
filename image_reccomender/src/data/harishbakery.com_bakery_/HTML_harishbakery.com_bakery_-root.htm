    About us

    Harish Bakery was started by Devender Nuhani and Surinder Chugh on 15th of May 1993 as a Bakery and Confectionery outlet in Gurgaon. In the last 20 years we have gained popularity and our outlet is now a ‘Common man Everyday’s Shop’.
